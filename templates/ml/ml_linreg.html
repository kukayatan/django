Linear Regression

In this case Linear Regression has been used to build the predictive model that would be able to predict the writing score of tested students with high accuracy. The dataset contains seven independet variables (math and reading score, gender, ethnicity, parental level of educationl, lunch, test preparation) and one dependent variable (writing score).

On the basis of further calculations, the model accuracy is over 94 % and the avarage error of 3.5 points.
